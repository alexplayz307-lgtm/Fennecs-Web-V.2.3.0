FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you need to know before commissioning or collaborating with Fennecs Dev Group.

Join our Discord server and open a commission ticket. We will discuss your project, timeline, and pricing there.

Join our Discord and message us in the collabs channel. Tell us about your project, your team size, and what kind of collaboration you are looking for. We review all requests.

Both. We handle animation-only commissions through Moon Animator and also do full development including Studio builds, scripting, and cinematic cutscenes.

Timelines depend on scope. Simple animation packs can be a few days. Full builds or game systems can take one to two weeks. We keep you updated throughout.

We expect clients to respect agreed timelines, communicate clearly, and follow standard commission TOS. We do not continue work with clients who are impatient, abusive, or who attempt to violate our commission terms.
